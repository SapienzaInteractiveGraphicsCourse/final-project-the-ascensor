Raccons Rush
Welcome to Raccons Rush game
You will impersonate Steve a young raccoon who wants to become the fastest raccoon in the world.
Today he will be able to fulfill his dream. He will be competing with the greatest raccoon racers from around the world.
All animals from all eras and all parts of the world have gathered today to witness this great event.
Will Steve be able to make his dream come true?
Instructions:
Press the A and D keys alternately to make Steve run, the faster you are the faster steve will be too.
Be careful though! If you don't click the keys alternately Steve will slow down.
During the race, some balloons will appear and, if you are fast enough to click them with the mouse, Steve will get a speed bonus
Choose difficulty:
Other raccoons are much slower than your maximum speed
Other raccoons are slower than your maximum speed, but not too much!
Also, it will be more difficult to reach the maximum speed
The speeds will be the same as the medium difficulty, only that the race will take place at night.
Therefore it will be more difficult to see the balloons to click on.
